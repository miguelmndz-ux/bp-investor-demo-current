Design decisions
What's new in this iteration and why it works.
What changed
1
Dark hero uses #1A0A00 — the deepest brown in our palette, not a generic black. Warm orange glow orbs keep it on-brand. The PH badge sits top-right of the Velo row.
2
Velo logo mark — "V" chevron in an orange gradient tile. Swap for the real Velo SVG/PNG in Stitch. The tile style works whether it's a wordmark or icon.
3
Copy from Option A — "We decoded your launch. Here's what we built for your demo." Orange highlight on "your demo" draws the eye to the personal angle.
4
Asset thumbnails lead with a color-coded preview band. Decode = blue-navy gradient. Course = green gradient. Real screenshots from /apex/ slot in here in Stitch.
5
"Accept Invite" CTA — full-width orange pill, same liquid glass style as the rest of the app. Sub-copy reduces friction: "No account needed."
6
Zero wasted space — no AI team section, no checklist. The email's single job: show the assets, book the slot. Everything else lives on the What to Expect screen.
Open question for you
Should the asset thumbnails show real screenshots of the Decode/Course pages (like a browser preview), or stay as these styled gradient cards? Real screenshots look more impressive but need the pages to be ready first.
