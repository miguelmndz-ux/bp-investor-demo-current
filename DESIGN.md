\# Design System Document

&#x20;

\## Theme Overview

This document outlines the core design tokens for our system, focusing on a vibrant and modern aesthetic.

&#x20;

\## Color Palette

The primary colors are derived from a vibrant orange, aiming for energetic and engaging user interfaces.

\- \*\*Primary Color\*\*: `#FF6B00` (A bold and distinctive orange, used for key actions and branding.)

\- \*\*Secondary Color\*\*: `#bd6001` (A supporting, slightly deeper orange, for secondary elements and accents.)

\- \*\*Tertiary Color\*\*: `#e5a102` (A bright, golden-orange accent for highlights and decorative details.)

\- \*\*Neutral Color\*\*: `#9f6c47` (A warm, earthy brown neutral for backgrounds and non-chromatic elements.)

The current color mode is \*\*light\*\*.

&#x20;

\## Typography

Our typography system uses a clean and modern sans-serif stack.

\- \*\*Headlines\*\*: `plusJakartaSans` (Strong and contemporary for titles and headings.)

\- \*\*Body Text\*\*: `inter` (Highly readable and versatile for all body copy.)

\- \*\*Labels\*\*: `inter` (Clear and concise for form labels and interactive elements.)

&#x20;

\## Shape and Form

UI elements feature \*\*maximum, pill-shaped\*\* roundedness for a soft and friendly appearance.

&#x20;

\## Spacing

The layout employs \*\*normal\*\* spacing, offering a balanced and comfortable visual experience.
